# Privacy Policy for PayAware

**Last updated:** 30 January 2026

PayAware respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data.

## 1. Information Collection
PayAware does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal information** to external servers.

- No account registration required
- No email, phone number, or personal identifiers collected
- No analytics or tracking tools used
- No third-party SDKs for data collection

## 2. Local Data Storage
All subscription and reminder data is stored **locally on your device only**.

- Data never leaves your device
- Data is not backed up to any server
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data

## 3. Permissions Usage
PayAware requests the following permissions:

### Notifications
Used to notify users about upcoming subscription renewals.

### Exact Alarm / Scheduled Alarm
Used to trigger reminders at precise times selected by the user.

These permissions are essential to the app’s core functionality.

## 4. Internet Usage
PayAware works completely **offline**.
No internet connection is required.

## 5. Third-Party Services
PayAware does not currently use advertisements, analytics, cloud services, or external APIs.
If third-party services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
PayAware does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.

## 7. Policy Updates
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
