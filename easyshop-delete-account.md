# EasyShop Account Deletion Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

EasyShop allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

## How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your EasyShop account, please send an email request to:

**[admin@easylifeglobal.app](mailto:admin@easylifeglobal.app)**

Include the following details in your email:

* **Registered Phone Number / Username**
* **Password**
* **Reason for account deletion (optional but recommended)**

## What Happens Next

After receiving your request:

1. Our team will verify the account details.
2. Your EasyShop account will be permanently deleted.
3. Associated account data will be removed from our systems.

## Data Deletion

Once the deletion request is processed:

* Your login credentials will be removed.
* Your account information will be deleted.
* Business data associated with your account may be removed unless retention is required for legal or operational purposes.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, please contact:

**[admin@easylifeglobal.app](mailto:admin@easylifeglobal.app)**

---

EasyShop is committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring users have control over their data.
